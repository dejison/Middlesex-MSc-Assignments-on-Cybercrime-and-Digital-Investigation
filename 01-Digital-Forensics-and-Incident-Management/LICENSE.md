## Data Protection & GDPR Compliance

All materials in this repository have been reviewed and redacted in accordance
with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection
Act 2018.

Redaction principles applied include:

- Data minimisation
- Purpose limitation
- Prevention of re-identification
- Ethical and harm-aware disclosure

Personal data, behavioural identifiers, operational details, and content
capable of enabling harm have been removed, summarised, or excluded entirely.
Only non-identifying technical artefacts and high-level analytical context
are retained.
