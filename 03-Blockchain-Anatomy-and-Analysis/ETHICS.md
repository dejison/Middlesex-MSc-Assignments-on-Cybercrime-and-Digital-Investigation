## Ethical and Legal Considerations

Although blockchain data is publicly accessible, this project
applies ethical constraints to avoid speculative attribution
or deanonymisation of individuals.

No attempt is made to identify real-world persons or entities
behind wallet addresses.



## Intended Use
This repository is intended for:

Academic assessment and coursework review
Demonstration of digital forensics methodology
Professional portfolio and skills presentation
Learning and teaching purposes only
Misuse of the material or attempts to reverse redactions are prohibited. See LICENSE.md for full terms.
